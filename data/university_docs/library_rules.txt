GTU / CampusAI Central Library Code of Conduct & Rules
Effective Date: July 01, 2026

The Central Library provides research and study resources for all registered students, researchers, and faculty.

1. Library Timings:
   - Monday to Saturday: 08:00 AM to 08:00 PM
   - Sundays and Public Holidays: Closed
   - During final examination periods, the Reading Hall is open 24/7.

2. Book Borrowing and Circulation Limits:
   - Undergraduate Students: Max 3 books for up to 14 days.
   - Postgraduate Students: Max 5 books for up to 21 days.
   - Reference books, current journals, and rare manuscripts cannot be borrowed and must be read in the library.

3. Late Fines and Renewals:
   - A fine of INR 10 per day is charged for books returned after the due date.
   - Borrowed books can be renewed once for an additional 7 days, provided there is no active reservation on the book.
   - Loss of a library book must be reported immediately. The student will be required to replace the book (latest edition) or pay double the cost of the book.

General Behavior Rules:
- Absolute silence must be maintained in the reading zones. Group discussion is permitted only in designated collaborative rooms (rooms L-201 and L-202).
- Carrying bags, personal books (unless stamped at the entry), umbrellas, and eatables is strictly prohibited inside the main library hall.
- Use of laptop computers is permitted, but audio must be muted or heard via headphones.

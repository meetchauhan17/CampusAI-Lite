GTU / CampusAI University Hostel & Mess Regulations
Revision: Version 2.4, 2026

Rules governing residential life in the campus boy's and girl's hostel blocks.

1. Hostel Timings and Curfew:
   - In-campus residents must return to the hostel blocks by 09:30 PM.
   - Out-of-campus permissions or night-outs require prior approval from the Chief Warden via the online hostel portal by 04:00 PM on the same day.
   - Daily roll-call is conducted by the Resident Wardens at 09:45 PM.

2. Mess Timings and Dining Hall Rules:
   - Breakfast: 07:30 AM to 09:00 AM
   - Lunch: 12:30 PM to 02:00 PM
   - High Tea / Snacks: 05:00 PM to 06:00 PM
   - Dinner: 07:30 PM to 09:00 PM
   - Dining hall utensils must not be taken to hostel rooms. Mess menus are updated on the first day of every month by the Mess Committee.

3. General Disciplinary Codes:
   - Use of heavy electrical appliances (heaters, induction cooktops, air conditioners) in rooms is strictly prohibited. Violation leads to confiscation and an INR 2,000 fine.
   - Visitors and parents are allowed only in the hostel common reception area between 04:00 PM and 07:00 PM. No visitor is allowed to stay overnight in student rooms.
   - Ragging, smoking, consumption of alcohol, or possession of banned substances is strictly prohibited and will lead to immediate expulsion from the hostel and suspension from the college.
